# Lab: Requirements for "Silent Bob" Coffee Shop for Introverts

---

## 2. Functional Requirements (FR)

*   **FR1: Order Management:** The system shall allow clients to select and order drinks via a mobile application without verbal interaction.
*   **FR2: Soundproof Entry:** The physical facility shall provide soundproof booths for clients to wait for their orders in isolation.
*   **FR3: AI Face Analysis:** The system shall analyze the client's facial expression via an onboard camera to determine "morning hatred" levels and automatically suggest coffee strength.
*   **FR4: Silent Delivery:** Orders shall be delivered via a dark, rotating airlock to eliminate any visual or physical contact between barista and client.
*   **FR5: Silence Enforcement:** If a barista makes any verbal sound (e.g., greeting), the system shall:
    *   Automatically deduct a fine from the barista's salary.
    *   Trigger the "Sad Bagpipe" audio file in the service area.
*   **FR6: Feedback Loop:** The mobile app shall include a "Complain about insufficient gloominess" button for user feedback.
*   **FR7: Elite Payments:** The system shall only accept cryptocurrency or rare postage stamps as valid payment methods.

---

## 3. Non-Functional Requirements (NFR)

*   **NFR1: Privacy & Anonymity:** The system must ensure that no human-to-human contact (visual, auditory, or physical) occurs during the entire process.
*   **NFR2: Acoustic Insulation:** Booths must provide at least 50dB of sound attenuation.
*   **NFR3: Security (Anti-Espionage):** The system must implement monitoring (audited by lawyers) to prevent the use of anonymous booths for illegal message exchanges, without violating the "introvert experience."
*   **NFR4: Scalability:** The AI analysis must process facial data in under 2 seconds.
*   **NFR5: Legal Compliance:** All biometric data processing must comply with regional privacy laws (GDPR/Data Protection acts).

---

## 4. Use Case: Ordering a Coffee

1.  **Actor:** Introvert Client.
2.  **Pre-conditions:** Client has the "Silent Bob" app and a crypto wallet/stamp.
3.  **Main Flow:**
    *   Client enters the soundproof booth.
    *   System scans client's face.
    *   AI suggests "Double Espresso" based on 90% morning hatred score.
    *   Client confirms and pays via Bitcoin.
    *   Barista prepares coffee in total silence.
    *   Order appears in the rotating airlock.
    *   Client retrieves coffee and leaves.
4.  **Exceptions:**
    *   Barista says "Enjoy!": System plays bagpipes and logs a fine.
